Calculate the latency in the wireless link

I am trying to calculate a theoretical network latency value when sending data over a network at a specific bitrate and FPS values

For example, I am wanting to stream data from a mobile to a wireless device on the same LAN, at a bitrate of 5 Mb/s at 90 frames per second. Assuming a network packet size of 1472 bits  = 1148 packets

data_rate=5*10**6 #bps

#streaming frames sending per second
FPS=90 
#size of each frame
Frame_size=18768 #bits
#number of bits streaming per second
number_of_bit_per_sec=FPS*Frame_size
print("number of bits streaming per second:",number_of_bit_per_sec)
packet_size=1472 #bits
number_of_pac=np.ceil(number_of_bit_per_sec/packet_size)
print("number of packet:",number_of_pac)
#time to send a packet
time_per_pac=packet_size/data_rate
print("time to send a packet",time_per_pac)
#delay time
delay_time=time_per_pac*2*number_of_pac
print(delay_time)
